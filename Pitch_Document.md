## Slide 2: The Problem (The Crisis in our Streets)
*   **Fragmented Logistics**: The current waste disposal system relies on disconnected players. Independent collectors burn fuel on empty routes, while households suffer from massive delays.
*   **Environmental Blindspot**: 70% of recyclable waste in emerging markets is dumped illegally or burned due to a lack of structured incentives for sorting at the source.
*   **Financial Friction**: Private waste operators struggle to scale because of heavy cash-handling issues, debt collections, and unpredictable revenue.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (EcoTrack)
EcoTrack is a centralized, digital logistics platform—the "Uber for Waste Management." 
We instantly bridge households and businesses with verified professional collectors using a real-time dispatch engine, bringing total transparency to the sanitation sector.

---

## Slide 4: How It Works
1. **Summon**: User opens EcoTrack, sets their waste volume (e.g., Organic, Plastics), and requests a pickup.
2. **Match & Track**: Our algorithm matches the request with the nearest verified collector. The user tracks the truck's approach in real-time via Google Maps.
3. **Pay & Earn**: Upon collection, fees are automatically deducted from the user's built-in digital wallet. The user earns EcoPoints for recycling, which they can instantly convert to mobile airtime or cash.

---

## Slide 5: The Business Model (How We Make Money)
1. **Transaction Margins**: We take a dynamic percentage fee (5-10%) on every digital wallet transaction exchanged between consumers and collectors.
2. **B2B Enterprise Subscriptions**: Malls, estates, and corporate bodies pay a flat monthly SaaS fee to manage their entire facility's waste logistics and generate automated ESG compliance reports.
3. **Data Monetization**: Selling aggregated, anonymized recycling volume data to FMCG companies and government agencies seeking to track supply-chain recovery rates.

## Slide 7: Our Competitive Advantage (Why We Win)
